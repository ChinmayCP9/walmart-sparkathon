Predictive Analysis Results for positive reviews and total sales

This program employs a linear regression analysis to forecast sales performance based on positive reviews. It begins by sourcing data from a designated CSV file, encompassing pertinent product attributes such as the count of favorable reviews and the corresponding sales figures over a three-month period. The data is stratified into a training set, utilized for model training, and a test set, employed to gauge model efficacy. A linear regression model is constructed to establish a mathematical relationship between positive reviews and sales, essentially capturing the underlying trends in the data. Following model calibration, its predictive capabilities are assessed using the test set, employing the root mean squared error as a metric for performance evaluation. This metric quantifies the disparity between predicted and actual sales, thus offering insight into the model's accuracy.
